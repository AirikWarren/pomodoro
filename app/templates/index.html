Welcome to tomatotimer.in! This tool was created to provide remote teams who are fans of the Pomodoro Technique with a simple, hassle free way to synchronize their Pomodoro timers.

The (really simple) directions

  1. Copy this link http://tomatotimer.in/room/YourTeamName
  2. Replace YourTeamName in the link with whatever you desire your (hopefully SFW) session name to be
  3. Share the link with your team via your normal means of communication

Yes, it's really that simple, and no, there isn't a catch. There's also no sign-ups, no charges, and no frills, in fact there's absolutely nothing on this website at all but a means to create a simple synchronized timer at an easy to remember domain. Efficient and purposeful, just like the teams that this tool was built for.
